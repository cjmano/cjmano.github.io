About Us

What we are

HSR Computer Education Ltd Courses cover A to Z in all areas of Software’s, Hardware’s, Networking and Multimedia. All the courses are industry relevant and future proof. The courses are designed to keep pace with the changing needs and times.


HSR Computer courses enable the students to prepare for the coveted worldwide certifications. Theoretical teaching is reinforced with practical training again and again. Guest lectures by experts from the Group and outside are periodically arranged for 100% industry exposure.


The specialty of HSR Computer Education Ltd Limited is 100% practical training. Along with the Computer Training, HSR Computer Education Ltd Limited also moulds the students with a unique personality development program, so that students face the real world without any problem.
